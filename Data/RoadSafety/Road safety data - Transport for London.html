Road safety data

Check our road danger reduction dashboards or find factsheets, reports, data extracts and other publications about road safety.

Road danger reduction dashboards

We have created interactive dashboards that let you explore the location and details of published collisions in London, inequalities in road danger and enforcement outputs from action taken by the police against traffic offences committed in London.

  • Road danger reduction dashboard
  • Inequalities in road danger dashboard
  • Vision Zero enforcement dashboard

These dashboards, and the reports below, can be used to self-serve data requests. For other casualty data email collisiondatarequests@tfl.gov.uk. For road enforcement output data enquiries email cposperformance@tfl.gov.uk.

Inequalities in road danger data

Below are the underlying data files which feed the Inequalities in road danger dashboard. There are three files, one containing collision records alongside data about the location of the collision, one containing collision records alongside data about the casualty's home location (for collisions where the casualty lives in London), and one containing LSOA area records alongside population, road length and deprivation information.
